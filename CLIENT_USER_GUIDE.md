## Reviewing Posts

### Opening a Post

1. Click on any post card in the calendar
2. A detailed view opens showing:
   - Full post copy
   - Scheduled date and time
   - Platform(s) it will post to (LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, etc.)
   - Images or videos
   - Your subsidiaries/brands mentioned

### What to Check

**✅ Review Checklist:**

1. **Post Copy** - Is the message clear and accurate?
2. **Scheduled Date** - Is this the right timing?
3. **Images/Videos** - Do visuals look professional and on-brand?
4. **Platform** - Should this post on these social channels?
5. **Links** - If there's a link, does it go to the right place?
6. **Hashtags** - Are hashtags appropriate and accurate?
7. **Brand Voice** - Does this sound like your company?

### Viewing Images

**Single Image:**
- Image appears in the post detail view
- Click to view full size

**Multiple Images (Carousel):**
- You'll see a 📸 carousel indicator
- Click the Box.com link to view all images

**Videos:**
- Click the Box.com link to view video

## Approving or Requesting Changes

### Approval Section

At the bottom of each post awaiting your review, you'll see:

**Three Options:**

1. **✅ Approve** - Green button
2. **🔄 Request Changes** - Red button
3. **💬 Comment Only** - Gray button

### How to Approve

If the post looks good:

1. Optionally add a comment (e.g., "Looks great!" or "Love this message")
2. Click **✅ Approve** button
3. Confirmation appears
4. Post status changes to "Approved"
5. FINN Partners team receives notification

**What Happens Next:**
- Post moves to production queue
- Will publish on scheduled date
- You'll see it change to "Scheduled" status

### How to Request Changes

If something needs to be updated:

1. **Add a comment** explaining what to change
   - Be specific: "Please change 'Q4' to 'Q1'"
   - Include context: "Image should show our new product, not the old one"
2. Click **🔄 Request Changes** button
3. Post returns to FINN Partners team as "Draft"
4. Team will make changes and resubmit for your approval

**Tips for Good Feedback:**
- ✅ "Please change the date in paragraph 2 to January 15"
- ✅ "Can we use a different image? This one doesn't show our new branding"
- ✅ "Love the message! Can we add our CEO's quote from the press release?"
- ❌ "This isn't right" (too vague)
- ❌ "Fix it" (doesn't say what to fix)

### Adding Comments Only

If you want to provide feedback without approving or rejecting:

1. Type your comment
2. Click **💬 Comment Only**
3. Post status doesn't change
4. FINN Partners team sees your feedback

**When to use:**
- You have a question
- You want to suggest an improvement but it's optional
- You're providing context for future posts

## Understanding Change History

Every post shows a "📜 Change History" section documenting all revisions.

### What You'll See

**Each Change Shows:**
- Date and time of change
- Who made it (FINN Partners team or you)
- What changed
- **New value** (green background) - what it was changed TO

**Change Type Badges:**
- **Agency Edit** - FINN Partners made this change
- **Client Edit** - You or your team made this change
- **Status Change** - Workflow update (submitted for review, approved, etc.)

### What Changes Are Tracked

**Content Changes:**
- Post copy edits
- Hashtag updates
- Note modifications
- Status changes (Draft → Awaiting Approval → Approved)

### Privacy Note

**What You See:**
- All versions FINN Partners has shared with you
- Changes made while post was awaiting your review or approved
- Final approved versions

**What You DON'T See:**
- Internal FINN Partners drafts and iterations
- Team brainstorming versions
- Changes marked as "internal only"

This ensures you see the relevant content history without overwhelming you with every internal edit.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### About Approvals

**Q: How quickly do I need to approve posts?**
A: We recommend reviewing within 24-48 hours. Check with your FINN Partners contact for specific deadlines.

**Q: What if I'm not sure about a post?**
A: Use **💬 Comment Only** to ask questions. Your FINN Partners contact will respond and help you decide.

**Q: Can I approve part of a post?**
A: No, it's all or nothing. If you want to change just one thing, use **🔄 Request Changes** and specify what to update.

**Q: What happens if multiple people from my company need to approve?**
A: All designated approvers must approve before the post moves forward. Check with your FINN Partners contact about who else is reviewing.

**Q: Can I un-approve a post?**
A: Contact your FINN Partners team immediately if you need to retract an approval. They can make changes and resubmit.

### About Content

**Q: Can I edit the post copy directly?**
A: Not in the portal. Use **🔄 Request Changes** with your edit, and FINN Partners will update it.

**Q: Can I change the scheduled date?**
A: Request date changes via **🔄 Request Changes**. Include the new date you prefer.

**Q: Can I see posts for next month?**
A: Yes! Use the ▶ arrow to navigate to future months.

**Q: Why can't I see some of our posts?**
A: You only see posts your FINN Partners team has submitted for your review. Internal drafts aren't visible until ready.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers work best?**
A: Chrome, Edge, and Safari (latest versions). Mobile browsers also work.

**Q: Do I need to install anything?**
A: No! It's a web portal - just click the link and sign in.

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**
A: Yes! The portal works on mobile devices, though the experience is optimized for desktop/tablet.

**Q: Why aren't images loading?**
A: Images may take a few seconds to load. If they don't appear after 10 seconds, try refreshing the page. Contact your FINN Partners team if issues persist.

**Q: What if I get an error message?**
A: Screenshot the error and send it to your FINN Partners contact. Try refreshing the page or using an Incognito/Private browsing window.

### Security & Access

**Q: Is this secure?**
A: Yes! Your portal is password-protected and you can only see your company's content.

**Q: Can I give access to colleagues?**
A: Contact your FINN Partners team to add additional users from your organization.

**Q: What if I forgot my login link?**
A: Email your FINN Partners contact to resend the portal link.

## Quick Reference

### Post Status Guide

| Status | What It Means | What You Do |
|--------|---------------|-------------|
| **Awaiting Your Approval** (Red) | Needs your review | Review and approve or request changes |
| **Approved** (Green) | You approved it | Nothing - FINN will publish |
| **Scheduled** (Blue) | Approved and queued | Nothing - will post automatically |
| **Published** (Purple) | Already posted | View for reference |

1. **Open post** - Click on calendar
2. **Review** - Check copy, images, date, platforms
3. **Decide**:
   - ✅ Approve if good to go
   - 🔄 Request changes if needs updates
   - 💬 Comment if you have questions
4. **Add comment** - Explain decision (optional for approve, required for changes)
5. **Click button** - Submit your decision
6. **Done!** - FINN Partners receives notification
